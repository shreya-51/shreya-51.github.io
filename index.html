Shreya Wadehra
Electrical Engineering, CS, and Physics

@ University of Pittsburgh, Swanson School of Engineering

Allegro

An accessible music recording tool for all musicians. Second place in University of Pittsburgh's Big Idea Blitz. Third place in University of Pittsburgh's Randall Family Big Idea Competition. Mentered through Blast Furnace Start-Up Accelerator.

Posture Protect

Wearable technology company aimed at helping Parkinson's patients with gait issues. Mentored through Blast Furnace Start-up Accelerator. Finalist in Randall Family Big Idea Competition 2020.

Endocompass

Designed an Arduino prototype of a gyroscopic endoscope for ENT surgeries. In collaboration with University of Pittsburgh Medical Center and Dr. Carl Snyderman.

4-20 mA Detector Interface

Developed an Arduino prototype of a touchscreen detector interface to be used onboard naval ships. In collaboration with the Naval Nuclear Laboratory.

EasyPEasy: A Tool for Adaptive Physical Education

Created a device to help teach motor skills to children with disabilities for the Western Pennsylvania School for Blind Children.

Interactive LED panel display

Developed a prototype of an interactive LED button display panel for the engineering building at the University of Pittsburgh, inspired by Google's AnyPixel.

The Pomodoro Watch

Ideated and developed a prototype of a Pomodoro-timer wearable device.

Color Coordination: Gamified Training

Ideated and developed an early prototype of a user interface with applications in physical therapy, exercise, and children's toys. Digital interface created using Python.

PhotonAlarm

Ideated and developed a prototype of a Bluetooth, light-connected alarm clock.
